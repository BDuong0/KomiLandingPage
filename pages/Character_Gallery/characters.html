Tadano Hitohito Character Information

Fun Facts

  • Tadano's mom has a French name (Jeanne) which is unusual in Japan
  • Hitohito's ability to "read the room" is borderline telepathy.
  • In the one-shot, his name was Tadano Shigeo.
  • His handle name is †JIN-JIN†.
  • Tadano's profile icon photo was a picture of a tomato.
  • Over time, he has become a close friend of Makoto, being able to see through his gruff exterior.
  • Hitohito makes a very convincing girl, a cute one at that. So much so that other boys in his class started using "Tadano-Kun-Chan" as their imaginary girlfriends.
  • During his chuunibyou phase, he often wore popped-up shirt collars and T-shirts with Sanskrit characters on them and would pose with his hands on his neck, as if it were hurting.
  • He likes old buildings of historical significance.
